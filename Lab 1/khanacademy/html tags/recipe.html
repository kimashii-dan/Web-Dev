Pizza Margherita

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Tomato Sauce 1 cup
Fresh Mozzarella 200g
Basil Leaves 8-10 leaves
Pizza Dough 1 ball

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).

Step 2: Roll out the pizza dough into a round shape.

Step 3: Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.

Step 4: Add mozzarella and basil leaves on top.

Step 5: Bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes until golden and crispy.

Sushi Rolls

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Cooked Sushi Rice 2 cups
Seaweed Sheets 4 sheets
Fresh Salmon 100g
Cucumber 1/2
Rice Vinegar 2 tbsp

Step 1: Prepare sushi rice and season with rice vinegar.

Step 2: Lay a seaweed sheet on a bamboo mat.

Step 3: Spread a thin layer of rice on the seaweed sheet.

Step 4: Add sliced salmon and cucumber, then roll tightly.

Step 5: Slice the roll into pieces and serve with soy sauce.

Grilled Steak

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Ribeye Steak 2 steaks
Olive Oil 2 tbsp
Garlic 2 cloves
Fresh Rosemary 2 sprigs
Salt and Pepper To taste

Step 1: Preheat the grill to high heat.

Step 2: Rub the steaks with olive oil, garlic, rosemary, salt, and pepper.

Step 3: Grill steaks for 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare.

Step 4: Let the steaks rest for 5 minutes before serving.
